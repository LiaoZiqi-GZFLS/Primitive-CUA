# wechat-follow-service-account-and-message

Open WeChat PC client, search for a service account, follow it, and send a private text message

## Prerequisites

WeChat PC client is installed with a desktop shortcut, the user account can log in (QR scan already completed or auto-login enabled), and the target service account name is known

## Steps

1. Double-click the WeChat desktop icon to launch the client
2. Click the '进入微信' (Enter WeChat) button to complete login
3. Click the search box at the top of the WeChat main window, paste the account name (e.g. '火眼审阅'), and press Enter
4. In the search results window, under the '账号' (Accounts) category, identify and click the entry whose type is '服务号' (service account) matching the name
5. On the service account profile page, click the green '关注' (Follow) button and verify it changes to '已关注' (Followed)
6. Click the '私信' (Message) button to open the chat window
7. Click the keyboard icon on the right side of the bottom toolbar to switch to text input mode
8. Paste the message text into the input field and click the '发送' (Send) button
9. Verify the message was sent successfully (appears as a green bubble with a timestamp)
