# wechat-follow-service-account-and-message

Launch WeChat PC client, search for and follow a service account (服务号), then send it a private text message with verification at each stage

## Prerequisites

WeChat PC client is installed with a logged-in (or quick-login-enabled) account; the target service account name is known; the message text to send is prepared

## Steps

1. Double-click the WeChat desktop icon to launch the client and wait ~3 seconds for it to load
2. Click the green 进入微信 (Enter WeChat) button to log in; take a screenshot to confirm the main chat list appears
3. Click the search box at the top of the chat list, paste the account name with paste_text, and press Enter to open the 搜一搜 (Search) results window
4. In the results, locate the account section and click the result explicitly labeled 服务号 (service account) — not other result types like 公众号 articles or mini programs
5. On the account profile page, click the green 关注 (Follow) button; screenshot to verify the button text changed to 已关注 (Following)
6. Click the 私信 (Private Message) button to open the chat window with the account
7. If a list_windows/focus_window step is needed, bring the chat window (titled with the account name) to the foreground
8. Click the keyboard icon at the far right of the bottom custom menu bar to switch from the account's custom menu to the text input field — this step is required before typing in service-account chats
9. Click into the input field and paste the message body using paste_text (handles Chinese text reliably)
10. Click the green 发送 (Send) button, then screenshot to verify the message appears as a sent green bubble with a timestamp

## Tools Used

click, wait, screenshot, set_mouse, list_windows, focus_window, paste_text
